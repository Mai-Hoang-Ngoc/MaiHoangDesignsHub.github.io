Exploring Emotional Spaces

The spaces that we inhabit and remember are important because they shape our experiences and are highly significant in our daily lives. These spaces evoke memories and emotions, and generate connections to people and events. One such special space for me is the conservatory in my family home. In this corner of the conservatory, one can sit and think, read or simply enjoy nature.

Background

The short project focused on our memory of a significant space, and the representation of that space through detailed inhabitation plan and model. As a result, I gained insight into sensory experiences and their impact on memory.
