The Flamingo House: Freelance Work

The project entailed the design of a four-bedroom residence in Dai Lai, Vietnam. The structural framework had already been established, so the task was to work within the existing load-bearing structure and adapt it to fulfil the clientâ€™s specific requirements.

Background

One crucial aspect of the project was the inclusion of an additional ground-floor bedroom and en-suite on the upper level. The client also expressed the desire for a spiral staircase leading to the rooftop, accessible from the main staircase and additional living space on the ground level. Additionally I actively participated in material sourcing and selection, engaging in meaningful discussions with suppliers.

The project adhered to a tight construction schedule, allowing me to cultivate my time-management and prioritisation abilities. It also provided valuable insights into the design process, emphasizing to me the importance of adaptability when working with clients.

Overall, this experience broadened my perspective on residential design, highlighting how even minor adjustments can profoundly impact our experience within a space.
