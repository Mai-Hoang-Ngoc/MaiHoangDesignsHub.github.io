The Centre For Ecology and Life : Visitors Centre

Project Brief: To design a community centre on the existing Bristol and Bath Rowing Club Site. Based on site research looking into the programs and needs of the site, I was able to choose what the community centre functioned as.

Background

In metropolitan areas such as Bristol and Bath, a noticeable disconnection from nature has taken root, particularly among the younger demographic. To truly appreciate and understand the natural world, it's essential to take a moment to slow down and closely observe it. This concept is mirrored in Saltford, where residents have the privilege of encountering nature through an array of semi-natural green spaces.

From extensive site research, it was decided that a visitors centre would best compliment the aims for the project. The scheme aims to educate Saltford's residents and visitors about the local ecology, including flora and fauna.

It achieves this by establishing a community center at the Bristol Rowing Club headquarters, repurposing materials from the existing boat storage warehouses into diverse functional blocks with classrooms, exhibition spaces, a bird hide, and a restaurant. Strong emphasis was put on utilising locally sourced and recycled materials, the project is responding to the context while upholding sustainability.
